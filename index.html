About Us

Introduction

National Service Scheme, abbreviated as NSS is a community-service oriented organisation launched in Gandhiji’s Centenary Year (1969) providing youth character building through community service.

NSS DCRUST, as a unit of NSS, involves students and faculty into nation building by organising various activities throughout the year. The NSS team organises a series of events by 5 different units, yet one can say it’s the most united unit in the College. NSS, DCRUST organises activities like imparting education to underprivileged children, organising T.B. , AIDS awareness fest, blood donation camps, visit to slums, visit to hospitals etc.
